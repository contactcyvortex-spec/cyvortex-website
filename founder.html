Vimal Sebastian

Founder & Cybersecurity Analyst – CyVortex

Vimal Sebastian is a young and passionate cybersecurity researcher from Alappuzha, Kerala. With a strong focus on ethical hacking, penetration testing, and OSINT investigation, he founded CyVortex to help individuals, students, and small businesses stay protected from modern cyber threats.



Biography

Starting his cybersecurity journey at a young age, Vimal trained rigorously in ethical hacking, Kali Linux, network security, digital forensics, and web application penetration testing. His goal is to build a cybersecurity ecosystem that is accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone — not just big companies.


Core Skills

  • ✔ Website & Web App Penetration Testing
  • ✔ OSINT & Digital Footprint Investigation
  • ✔ Network & WiFi Security Analysis
  • ✔ Vulnerability Assessment (VAPT)
  • ✔ Linux & Kali Linux Operations
  • ✔ Secure Website Development
  • ✔ Report Writing & Remediation Guidance

Vision & Mission

Vimal’s mission is to increase cyber awareness and provide powerful, affordable, and real-world security services. He aims to expand CyVortex into a recognized cybersecurity brand that supports startups, students, and businesses globally.
