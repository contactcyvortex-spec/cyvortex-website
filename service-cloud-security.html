Cloud Security Audit

A detailed security evaluation of your cloud environment to identify misconfigurations, access-control weaknesses, and data exposure risks. We assess IAM roles, storage buckets, firewall rules, network permissions, encryption policies, exposed services, and cloud resource configurations across platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Our audit detects vulnerabilities such as overly permissive access, unsecured APIs, unprotected databases, weak policies, and logging gaps. You receive a clear, actionable report with risk levels, screenshots, and recommended fixes to harden your cloud infrastructure and ensure data protection, compliance, and strong overall security posture..
