About CyVortex

CyVortex is a cybersecurity brand built with one mission — to make professional-grade security services available to everyone. We focus on students, freelancers, startups, remote workers, and small businesses who deserve reliable digital protection at an affordable cost.

Our Mission

To make world-class cybersecurity accessible, affordable, and understandable for individuals and businesses across the world. We aim to deliver top-tier pentesting, OSINT, and digital protection services with transparency, trust, and real human support.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted student-led cybersecurity brand — building a safer digital ecosystem where every person has the knowledge, tools, and confidence to defend themselves from modern cyber threats.

Our Founder

Vimal Sebastian — Founder & CEO, CyVortex

A passionate cybersecurity learner turned entrepreneur, Vimal founded CyVortex with the purpose of making security services accessible and trustworthy for everyone.

“I started CyVortex because security should be accessible to every student, freelancer, and business — not just big tech companies.”
