Network & WiFi Security Testing

A full security assessment of your internal network, routers, switches, firewalls, and Wi-Fi access points. We analyze the strength of your wireless encryption, detect weak passwords, identify open ports, misconfigurations, unauthorized devices, and potential entry points used by attackers. Our testing includes network mapping, MITM (Man-in-the-Middle) simulation, rogue access point detection, and Wi-Fi cracking checks. You receive a detailed report with all vulnerabilities, risks explained in simple language, and recommended fixes to harden your network and make your Wi-Fi infrastructure fully secure and intrusion-resistant..
